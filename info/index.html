House Info

Check-out Instructions:

Dishes

• Place all dirty dishes in the dishwasher and start cleaning cycle

Bedsheets

• Strip used linens and leave on top of bed for cleaning staff

Outdoor couch & Hammock

• Place couch pillows and hammocks back in storage bin (south porch)

Hot tub

• Ensure hot tub cover straps are securely closed

Lock up

• Ensure all exterior doors are locked (lounge/TV room & laundry room)
• Lock front door by pressing the "August" logo on keypad

Pets

• There is a strict no-pet policy

Plumbing (septic)

• Do NOT flush feminine hygiene products down toilets
• Please use trashcans provided in the bathrooms

Trash

• Kitchen: garbage and recycle under the sink (to the right)
• Large garbage can in the laundry room

Safety

• Fire extinguisher located in laundry room
• First aid kits are in top-drawers of entry buffet at bottom of stairs

Small children

• Pack 'n' play is in the downstairs master bedroom closet

Furnace/Fireplaces

• DO NOT change any setting on the furnace itself
• Set the thermostat in the room,
• Corresponding unit will adjust to temperature.

Operating Showers

• Turn on: move larger handle in either direction
• Colder: Turn smaller center knob clockwise
• Hotter: Turn smaller center knob counter-clockwise
• Even Hotter: Push small center knob in (towards wall)
• Continue turning counter-clockwise

Radiant heating in bathrooms

• We kindly ask that guests don't turn these on

Cooking

• Stovetop is induction. Use the provided stainless steel cookware

Outdoor Couch & Hammocks

• Pillows and hammocks are located in storage bin on south porch
• There is a hammock hanging in its case on a tree south (water-side) of the house as well
